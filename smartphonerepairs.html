Smartphone Repairs

We use our smartphones on a daily basis to shop, bank, work, communicate and capture precious memories—but having a broken screen, a dead battery, or a phone that won’t hold a charge can mean a lot of unwanted disruption in our routines. SolutionByDivine’s goal is to streamline virtually every kind of repair and make it as smooth as possible to get you back on track, and to get your phone looking brand new. We accept both walk-ins and appointments and use only original parts, with the average repair taking our highly skilled technicians less than 30 minutes to complete. If you’re looking to get yours fixed, stop our shop today! All repairs come with our 90-days Warranty.
